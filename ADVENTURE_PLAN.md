# Adventure Game Plan

## Overview

You are an explorer entering the mysterious City of Echoes. Your choices determine whether you uncover its secret, escape, or get trapped.

## Story Paths

### Path 1: The Scholar's Route

- **Decision Point 1:** At the city gate
  - **Choice A:** Enter through the main gate → leads to library district.
  - **Choice B:** Sneak through a side alley → leads to shadow market.

- **Decision Point 2 (Main gate → library district):**
  - **Choice A:** Talk to the old librarian → learn about the Echo Core.
  - **Choice B:** Ignore the librarian and explore alone → risk getting lost.

- **Decision Point 2 (Side alley → shadow market):**
  - **Choice A:** Buy a mysterious map → shortcut to Echo Core.
  - **Choice B:** Refuse the map → encounter city guards.

### Path 2: The Rogue's Route

- **Decision Point 1:** At the city wall
  - **Choice A:** Climb the wall → rooftop chase.
  - **Choice B:** Bribe a guard → secret tunnel.

- **Decision Point 2 (Rooftop chase):**
  - **Choice A:** Jump across a broken bridge → dangerous but fast.
  - **Choice B:** Take a safer detour → slower but safer.

- **Decision Point 2 (Secret tunnel):**
  - **Choice A:** Follow the glowing symbols → leads to hidden chamber.
  - **Choice B:** Ignore symbols → tunnel collapse risk.

### Path 3: The Mystic's Route

- **Decision Point 1:** At the forest outside the city
  - **Choice A:** Follow the whispers → spirit guide appears.
  - **Choice B:** Ignore whispers → enter city alone.

- **Decision Point 2 (Spirit guide):**
  - **Choice A:** Trust the guide → direct path to Echo Core.
  - **Choice B:** Doubt the guide → alternate, confusing route.

- **Decision Point 2 (Enter city alone):**
  - **Choice A:** Seek a temple → learn ancient ritual.
  - **Choice B:** Wander the streets → risk being trapped in echoes.

## Endings

- **Ending A:** You harness the Echo Core and leave as its guardian.
- **Ending B:** You escape but never uncover the secret.
- **Ending C:** You are trapped in the City of Echoes forever.
